## Detailed Description (For Product Page)

### The Problem

If you're building a SaaS product, you're drowning in feedback:
- App store reviews
- Support tickets  
- NPS survey responses
- Social media mentions
- Feature request emails

It's scattered across 5+ tools, and finding patterns feels impossible. You spend hours each week just organizing feedback—time that could be spent building.

### The Solution

FeedbackLens aggregates all your feedback in one place and uses GPT-4 to:
- **Analyze sentiment** — Know how users really feel
- **Detect themes** — See what topics come up most
- **Surface insights** — Get actionable recommendations
- **Track trends** — Monitor sentiment over time

### How It Works

1. **Connect your sources** — Reviews, support tickets, surveys
2. **AI analyzes everything** — Sentiment, themes, urgency
3. **Get your dashboard** — Insights at a glance
4. **Export to your tools** — Notion, Linear, Jira

### Built For

- SaaS founders who need to understand users fast
- Product managers overwhelmed by feedback volume
- Customer success teams tracking sentiment trends
- Anyone who wants to build products users love
